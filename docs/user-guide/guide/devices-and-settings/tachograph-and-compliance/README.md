# Tachograph and compliance

For tachograph-capable vehicles, these blocks let you **remotely retrieve digital tachograph data as DDD files** and have them emailed automatically — supporting compliance with driving-time and rest-period regulations.

## What are DDD files?

DDD files store detailed information from a digital tachograph about driver activities, including driving hours, rest periods, and potential compliance violations. The frequency of downloading and the retention period are regulated by law. In the EU, for example, DDD files are typically downloaded about every month from the driver's card and about every three months from the vehicle unit, with a mandatory storage period of about one year.

{% hint style="warning" %}
Navixy supports the **download functionality** of DDD files. We do not process them in any way and do not read any data from them.
{% endhint %}
