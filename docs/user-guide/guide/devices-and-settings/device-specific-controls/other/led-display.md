# LED display

Controls the device's status LED indicator.

## Settings

* **LED mode**: choose between two modes:
  * **Switched on for 10 seconds after pressing the sensor button**: the LED activates briefly when the button is pressed, then turns off.
  * **Always switched on, except in power-save mode**: the LED stays on continuously, except when the device enters a low-power state.

## Availability

Appears on device models that expose LED control.
