# How to integrate your devices

Navixy makes it effortless to connect your GPS and IoT devices — no matter the brand, model, or protocol.

Whether you’re using a Teltonika in South America, a Queclink in Asia, or a custom-built sensor network, Navixy’s platform unifies them all into one seamless system. Our device-agnostic architecture ensures you can focus on outcomes, not compatibility headaches.

### One universal language: The Navixy Generic Protocol (NGP)

At the heart of Navixy’s integration power is NGP, the [Navixy Generic Protocol](https://www.navixy.com/iot-logic/navixy-generic-protocol/) — our open, universal “language” for telematics data.

* Works with any modern GPS or IoT hardware
* Standardizes all incoming and outgoing data
* Freely available for manufacturers to adopt
* Enables instant compatibility and easy device onboarding

If your device sends data — chances are, it already integrates with Navixy.

And if it doesn’t yet, our IoT Gateway can translate any proprietary protocol into NGP, preserving your existing hardware investments while modernizing your data pipeline .

### Connect everything — From OEM Telematics to IoT Sensors

Navixy integrates OEM modules, aftermarket devices, and next-gen IoT sensors in one interface.

* OEM Telematics — Connect data streams from systems like GM OnStar, Ford Pro, or Volvo Connected Vehicle, and see diagnostics directly on your dashboard.
* IoT Sensors — Integrate wireless temperature tags, fuel sensors, or Bluetooth beacons — perfect for logistics and asset tracking.
* Satellite & LPWAN — Bring in remote assets through satellite, LoRaWAN, or private networks without losing a single data point.

Navixy’s store-and-forward logic ensures no signal is lost, even in low-connectivity environments .

### Fast, deep, and automated integration

We believe adding a new device shouldn’t take weeks. With Navixy, it often takes just days.

#### What makes it fast

* Automated provisioning and SIM configuration
* Remote management — update firmware, change settings, or activate devices over the air
* Health monitoring tools for instant troubleshooting

#### What makes it deep

Navixy doesn’t just show position and speed — it unlocks every feature your hardware provides:

* Fuel level and theft alerts
* Driver ID and access control
* Live video and ADAS events
* Tire pressure, temperature, and other telemetry

That means you get maximum value from your hardware — nothing left unused.

### No vendor lock-in. Total freedom.

Navixy was designed to eliminate lock-in. You can freely mix and match devices from any manufacturer — and change them anytime — without re-architecting your software.

* Choose the best hardware for each region or task
* Avoid hidden OEM fees or data restrictions
* Keep full ownership and control of your telemetry data

This flexibility gives businesses confidence to scale, experiment, and innovate — knowing their platform will adapt, not constrain them .

### Scalable and future-ready

With millions of assets managed globally, Navixy is built for scale. Our cloud-based architecture handles millions of daily events from diverse devices while staying fast, reliable, and globally accessible.

And because our system is protocol-extensible, it’s ready for whatever comes next — from EV telemetry to AI-powered sensors.

In short: Navixy grows as your business grows .
